### John Buttrick of Union Square Ventures and Sean Moran of eShares join Nick to cover The Cap Table.

In this two-part episode, we discuss:

- What a cap table is and what to expect on most cap tables
- Common issues when reviewing a cap table
- Why there's a need for an automated solution
- The value for investors of a clean and potentially automated cap table
- The startup's perspective on cap table management
- Employee equity and transparency
- The market opportunity for cap table software
- Data opportunities in private company ownership
